Creative Endeavors and Coding

I absolutely love to create. Whether from paint and paper, fabric and thread, or lines of code and a computer, making something beautiful and cohesive from where there was once nothing is more than a hobby for me. I spend much of my free time writing, making art, and putting together costumes all while constantly challenging myself to learn and integrate new techniques to better the quality and intricacy of my creations. When it's time to hop into developer mode, I keep this mindset with me and take special care to foster my inquisitive nature when I program. I'm always eager for more exposure to the ever-growing mediums available for swatching on the canvas of computer science so that I might have the opportunity to apply them to my own work!
